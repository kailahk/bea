BEA KORSH is a typeface designer living in Brooklyn, NY.

Bea enjoys the privilege of working for accomplished typeface designers like David Jonathan Ross↗ & Christian Schwartz.↗ She also designs her own typefaces, which are informed by drum kits↗ and comics↗ as much as they are by letterform history and convention. She graduated from the Type@Cooper Extended Program↗ in 2022 and now works there as a TA.
